Governance Framework

Leadership & Institutional Oversight

SportsCongo is governed by a framework designed to ensure institutional discipline, financial transparency, and commercial rigor. Our leadership model is structured around four critical pillars of expertise that oversee all continental and diaspora operations.

Executive Management

Responsible for the day-to-day strategic deployment of capital and asset scaling across diverse jurisdictions.

Strategic Advisory Board

A council of industry veterans providing guidance on regulatory alignment and global sports policy.

Finance & Risk

Rigorous oversight of capital allocation, project financing, and institutional risk management frameworks.

Legal & Governance

Ensuring compliance across international media rights law, local sports acts, and continental trade agreements.

Commercial Ops

Driving the monetization strategy through sponsorship, broadcast distribution, and ticketing infrastructure.

Technical Excellence

Developing the physical and digital infrastructure required to host and broadcast world-class sports events.
